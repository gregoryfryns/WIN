Women in Neuroscience

Female neuroscientists are still under-represented in various neuroscience conferences (biaswatchneuro). Identifying suitable female speakers for conferences can be a challenge, despite some initiatives (anneslist, 500womenscientists). The aim of this project is to promote female neuroscientists through a repository and recommendations (work in progress) provided by senior researchers.

* Update: the entries of the "Women in MEEG" list have been added to this repository. Thanks to its contributors: Virgine van Wassenhove, Jennifer Groh and Anne Churchland!
