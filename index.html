Women in Neuroscience

Female neuroscientists are still under-represented in various neuroscience conferences (biaswatchneuro). Identifying suitable female speakers for conferences can be a challenge, especially as some women get invited to most events (and must decline) and some are never heard of. Initiatives exist (anneslist, 500womenscientists) and they are great resources. However, it can feel risky to invite someone that we never saw speak or do not know from publications. The aim of this project is to create a repository of female neuroscientists with recommendations. Recommendations are provided by senior researchers (senior scientist, lecturer, associate/assistant prof, group leader or director), detailing why a person should be considered as speaker.

To add your name to the list please fill this Google form. Please note that researchers are sole responsible for their information and we do not guarantee authenticity.
